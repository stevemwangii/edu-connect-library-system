FAQ'S

Q: What is EduConnect Library?

This is an Open-Source Library Website that contains a Resources section to learn different topics, Donate book section to donate your old books, and a Book issue section to keep a record of all the books issued.

Q: What is the best part of EduConnect Library?

The best part about the website is that you can also contribute to the website for others to use the resourses by Uploading the books/documents you have.

Q: How to become a member?

By registering , you can become a member of the EduConnect Library.

Q: What is the registration fee?

There is no registration fees! One simply needs to register to become a member.

Q: I forgot my login credentials. What should I do?

No worries! Click on the "Forgot your Password?" link. An email containing the instructions to reset your password will be sent to your registered email address.

Q: I can't find the resource i am looking for. What to do?

The library has a huge collection of books and resources of multiple genres. However, if you can't find the book you're looking for, do reach out to us! Drop us a message through the Contact us section.

Q: What are the benefits of an account in EduConnect-Library?

  1. Contribute to EduConnect-library : EduConnect-library is a community initiative to build a robust book catalog. Anyone can add or edit data about books and blogs.
  2. Personalize your experience : We are always adding new features to our site so you can make your experience more personal.

Q: How can I find documents or journals here?

Go to the Choose and read books section and search the book you are looking for.

Q: I get an error saying unsupported format ?

This is not supposed to happen but if it happens please leave a message on our or change format to PDF Contact us section. We will definitely look into it.

Q: How can I download a document?

Go to our Recommended Book section. Choose the book you want to download then click on the "Download as pdf" button. Happy reading :)

Q: How many files can I add at once?

You can add as many documents as you want.

Q: How can I access the Online library?

Go to our Resources section. Click on the "All resources" button. You can find in our environment all recources to satisfy your need.
